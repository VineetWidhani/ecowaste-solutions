Recycling Solutions

Our state-of-the-art recycling facilities process a wide range of materials including plastics, metals, paper, and glass. We use advanced sorting technologies to maximize material recovery and ensure the highest quality recyclables.

We work with businesses, municipalities, and communities to implement effective recycling programs that reduce waste and create value.

  • Single-stream and multi-stream recycling options
  • Material recovery rates up to 95%
  • Certified recycling processes
  • Regular collection and pickup services
  • Detailed recycling reports and analytics
🌱

Composting Programs

Transform your organic waste into nutrient-rich compost that can be used for landscaping, agriculture, or sold as a premium soil amendment. Our composting solutions help reduce methane emissions from landfills while creating valuable products.

From food scraps to yard waste, we handle all types of organic materials with our industrial-scale composting facilities.

  • Food waste and green waste processing
  • Commercial and residential programs
  • Premium compost production
  • Odor-free collection containers
  • Educational workshops and training
💻

E-Waste Collection

Electronic waste requires special handling to protect both the environment and your sensitive data. Our certified e-waste program ensures proper disposal of electronics while recovering valuable materials like gold, silver, and rare earth elements.

We provide secure data destruction services with certificates of destruction for your records.

  • Computers, phones, and IT equipment
  • Certified data destruction (NIST standards)
  • Batteries and hazardous component handling
  • Asset tracking and reporting
  • Pickup and drop-off options available

Sustainability Consulting

Our expert consultants help organizations develop and implement comprehensive sustainability strategies. From waste audits to zero-waste roadmaps, we provide the guidance you need to achieve your environmental goals.

We work with you to identify opportunities, set targets, and measure progress towards a more sustainable operation.

  • Comprehensive waste audits and assessments
  • Zero-waste strategy development
  • Employee training and engagement programs
  • Sustainability reporting and metrics
  • Regulatory compliance assistance
